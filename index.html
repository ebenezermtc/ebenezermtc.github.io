Details of Meditation blog  

Please visit http://www.mtcreflection.com

www.mtcreflection.com is a blog implemented by the IT Fellowship to publish the daily meditations of Christian Education Forum. This blog has all the meditations published so far. It is available in mobile format too which is good for people travelling. This blog offers free subscription. Those who are interested in getting the meditations in email format can subscribe to this by entering their email id in the subscription field of the blog. Creating a link to this blog on the parish website will also help people to access this. Contact Mr. Reji Joseph, NJ , (201) 647-3836 with more questions.
