"But you, Bethlehem, in the land of Judah, are by no means least among the rulers of Judah; for out of you will come a ruler who will shepherd my people Israel."

My Beloved in Christ.

First of all I wish a happy Christmas greetings to all my beloved parish members.

It is wonderful time to celebrate Christmas. We are meditating the incarnation of our Savior Jesus Christ. So the Church is observing these time as Lent (i.e. December1st to 25th). The Father Almighty sends His Son to keep us safe from the sinful life. He came for all universe. The Purpose was Resumption and Adoption .The divine birth of Jesus was not accidental. It is purpose and the plan by God."The Angel said, "Thou shall call His name Jesus, for He shall save them which were lost. The Word of God says God so loved the world. So If we accept His son wholeheartedly we will be save by the Christ.

Every Christmas should be an encounter with Jesus. If we search for Jesus with our worldly knowledge and wisdom we will surely miss him. Lord Jesus Christ declared that "Blessed are the poor in spirit for theirs is the kingdom of heaven. We must become poor and lowly or we must come out of our self-exalting spirit and then only can we find Jesus. We can visualize his Birth and experience all its glory of our homes.

May the love, joy and peace of our Lord be with you.

HAPPY CHRISTMAS AND HAPPY NEW YEAR.

YOURS

BENJAMINACHEN
