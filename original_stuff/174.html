Dearly Beloved in Christ,    

              YOU SHALL NOT MURDER!

 

Plants, insects, all creatures, great and small are interdependent balancing each other in an ecological phenomenon! God looks upon all of life on the planet as something with infinite potential. Any fool can count the seeds in an apple, but only God can count all of the apples in one seed”. God values the life potential in each life and in each seed. Give us one fertile seed and which will give you a garden, an orchard, an industry or a family”. If you can’t plant a garden, be one your hart can be the soil: Christ’s love, the flower, this spirit the fragrance

Some men die in shrapnel. Some men die in flame, most men perish inch by inch playing at little games. Yes, we all die. By disaster or disease, by guns or germs. By crashes or cancer some die by growing old. Too many others die by pathetically losing the will to live.  Only a very few will be murdered. Few, if any, readers of this line need to be told it is a sin too. Respect life! Reverence life! Restore life, Revive life, and Release life!

Respect life- means don’t destroy your own life or anyone else’s. Don’t undervalue our own self. Self-esteem - when we lose our self-esteems we lose the ability to be possibility thinkers. When we cannot cope, we lose hope when we lose hope the will to die takes over, and we’ll want to die. Even if we don’t attempt suicide we may self –destruct through a degenerate life style we all know that the alcoholic and those who seek relied with chemical addictions are really choosing a lifestyle that’s sold-destructive. They kill themselves by killing their awareness of the world around them. Don’t undervalue ourselves. Upgrade the potential worth of our own life. Give ourselves credit for being intelligent creative inventive.

Exercise our freedom to dream dreams, set goals and make commitments. We have the potential to handle difficulties we may face in relationship, marriage, business, Schools or even in our own health. Value ourselves and our life highly. A person who treats himself shabbily will treat others shabbily too. 

Restore life: Begin by practicing forgiveness. This miracle power will always work wonders- Why heavy luggage have you been carrying too long?  And at what price?

Old sorrows? It’s time to bury the dead. Old sins? It’s time to accept Christ’s forgiveness. Old Setbacks?. It’s time to believe in beginning again. Old insults? It’s time to let the past be the past. Old hurts and Angers? It’s time to accept the spirit of forgiveness.

It is more inner strength to forgive than it does to inflict revenge. Forgiveness need not imply compromise with wrong doing. Only the Strong, Self-assured, Self-confident will have the inner power and patience to become “peace makers”.

Discover the pride of becoming a peacemaker. Now quite possibly you can build a bridge of communication with the enemy. Together conflict resolutions can begin.

Ego power becomes eagle power, when it lifts us above the levels of those who do damage to our dignity.

You shall be called the Repairer of the Breach. The Restorer of Streets to Dwell in.

                                                                                                                                 (Isa: 58:12)

Revive Life: - Jesus said “ whoever is angry without a cause commits murder in his heart”.

Anger- what is it really, this strong surge of a sometimes silent, sometimes shouting emotion that generates such as incredible force in mood, mind, manner, mouth and muscle?  Anger is human- Dignity violated        

 

                                                        May God Bless Us All,

                                                                                                Sujan Achen
